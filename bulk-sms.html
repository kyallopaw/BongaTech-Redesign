Features

With our bulk SMS solutions, all the target GSM active phone number users are reached by single click in a matter seconds.  The features of Bongatech Bulk SMS solutions are:

SMS Inbox

We offer both 1 way and 2-way SMS. Receive all incoming SMS messages or replies from your users in one place. Inbox available.

Birthday SMS

Import an Excel of your clients birthdays and customize the greetings. Take advantage, and send them personalized greetings while building brand loyalty thanks to SMS Birthday.

SMS Outbox

Contains all sent SMS, SMS that have failed and also all pending SMS that are to be delivered at later time.

Desktop portal

A desktop application with complete set of features to equip you for any kind of mobile messaging activity or campaign.

Scheduled SMS

Schedule SMS text messages. This feature is perfect for when you know you will not be at your desk and an SMS message needs to go out.

User Profile

Display account information. Modify contact details. View account top up and billing information. Add new sub accounts. Change actual password.
